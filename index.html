Practical Safety Tips

Implement these actionable strategies to enhance your digital safety today.

Protect Your Privacy

Regularly review privacy settings on social media and apps. Limit the personal information you share online, especially location data, birthdates, and contact details.

Strengthen Security

Use unique, complex passwords for each account and enable two-factor authentication wherever possible. Keep your software and devices updated with the latest security patches.

Combat Cyberbullying

If you witness cyberbullying, don't participate or share harmful content. Support the target, report the behavior to platform moderators, and save evidence of the harassment.

Audit Your Digital Footprint

Periodically search your name online to see what information is publicly available. Request removal of outdated or inappropriate content from websites and adjust your privacy settings accordingly.
