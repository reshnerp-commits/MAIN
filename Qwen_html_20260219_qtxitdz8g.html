Essential Privacy Practices

Simple but powerful steps to enhance your digital security today

  • 1
    Use Strong, Unique Passwords

    Create complex passwords for each account and use a password manager to store them securely.

  • 2
    Enable Two-Factor Authentication

    Add an extra layer of security to your accounts with 2FA via authenticator apps or SMS.

  • 3
    Review App Permissions

    Regularly check which apps have access to your location, contacts, camera, and microphone.

  • 4
    Limit Personal Info Sharing

    Be cautious about what you post publicly on social media and avoid oversharing sensitive details.

  • 5
    Use Privacy-Focused Browsers

    Switch to browsers like Firefox or Brave that block trackers and respect your privacy by default.

  • 6
    Encrypt Sensitive Data

    Use encryption for important files and communications, especially when using public Wi-Fi.

Privacy Settings Guide

Step-by-step instructions for major platforms

Google Account Privacy Settings

Control what Google knows about you and how your data is used.

  1. Go to myaccount.google.com and sign in
  2. Navigate to Data & Privacy in the left menu
  3. Review History settings: Turn off Web & App Activity, Location History, and YouTube History if desired
  4. Under Ad personalization, click "Go to ad settings" and turn off personalized ads
  5. Visit Third-party apps & services to revoke access for apps you no longer use
  6. Enable 2-Step Verification under Security for added protection
